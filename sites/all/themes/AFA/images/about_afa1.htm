About AFA

 

Chase Chenowith

AMERICAN FILM ACADEMY is the creation of multiple Award Winning Producer and Screenwriter Terry Chase Chenowith.  A twenty-five year veteran of film, television, reality-tv, television commercials, music production and live music productions.

Chenowith is also the Author of the book “JOBS IN THE MOVIES,” which is about to be published.

Terry Chase Chenowith is on the Board of Directors of the Central Florida Film Festival and has won 11 Best Picture Awards worldwide, and 15 total Awards worldwide.

He has produced films with Anthony Hopkins, Naomi Watts, Alec Baldwin, Jennifer Love Hewitt, Dan Ackroyd, Christopher Walken, the writers of “Alien, “Cinderella Man, “Good Morning Vietnam, “Total Recall” and many more.  Now he brings his experience to you.
